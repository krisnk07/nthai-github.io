The Growth in Technology

In the era of technology rapid advancement, "Artificial Intelligence" is a crucial component. These days, artificial intelligence technology is regarded as one of the most significant technology industries, contributing user in resolving a variety of task and problem in life. Thanks to advancements with incredibly contemporary and remarkable qualities, this field is receiving a lot of attention on a global scale.

What is AI?

Within the field of computer science is artificial intelligence (AI). It is the technology of simulating human thinking and learning processes for machines, especially computer systems. Self-correction, reasoning (applying rules to arrive at approximate or definitive conclusions), and learning (acquiring information and rules for employing the information) are some of these processes.

AI applications potentially in use

AI is utilized in today's urban areas to enhance operations, management effectiveness, and user experience across a wide range of fields, including transportation, education, healthcare, business and production, the arts, content creation, etc. A significant turning point in society was marked with the creation of ChatGPT in 2022 (Forbes, ChatGPT history) with countless opportunities like automate operations such as coding, computer design, content production, product brainstorming, and text drafting and summarization.

Ethics of artificial intelligence

AI tools give users access to a broad spectrum of new potential, however the Copyright of AI-generated products remains controversial.The tools reflect what it have learned, meaning that these algorithms are heavily dependent on the data it had trained on.
Some of the ethical challenges of AI include:
-Misuse of AI leads to fake, scam, and other harmful content.
-Copyright, plagiarism issues.
-Increasing unemployment due to the growing use of AI to automate tasks in the workplace.
